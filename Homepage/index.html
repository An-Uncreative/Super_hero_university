Welcome to G2

You are welcome to G2 Academy for superheroes, a top ranking superhero college in the whole world. Established in 2020, we were tasked with educating and training individuals that were gifted abilities due to the covid-19 pandemic. Since then, we have grown tremendously, now able to admit students from all major power categories.

VISION

To be able to admit students from all categories, including branched ones.

MISSION

To create a norm for superheroes in our society by training our students to use their power for good always.
